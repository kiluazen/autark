What actually happens
An agent for customer acquisition.

Most tools help you build the product. Autark starts after that. It turns a live product into a live customer-finding loop.

Starts with what you already have A product brief, a landing page, and a clear buyer outcome are enough to begin.
Finds customers instead of making plans about customers It picks an ICP, tests channels, sends messages, and learns from what comes back.
Keeps iterating until the market starts talking back Better ICP, better message, better surfaces. The loop tightens every pass.
How it works
1. Understand the product
Autark reads the product brief, landing page, and buyer outcome so it knows what is being sold and why anyone would care.
2. Pick the best ICP
It starts with the smallest believable group of people who seem most likely to need the product now.
3. Find where they are
Communities, inboxes, bylines, directories, niche social surfaces, and anywhere else the target buyer actually shows up.
4. Send the right outreach
Email is not Reddit and Reddit is not LinkedIn. The message changes with the channel instead of getting sprayed everywhere.
5. Learn from the response
Replies, silence, objections, and unexpected wins all become signal for the next pass instead of dead notes in a doc.
6. Repeat until it clicks
The point is not one campaign. The point is a recursive system for finding customers until a real demand pattern appears.
